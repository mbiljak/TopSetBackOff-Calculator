The Program
Day A Heavy Day
Sets
1
Reps
5
Top Set — Work up to a heavy 5. Leave 1 rep in the tank. This is your benchmark for the day.
Rest 3–5 min
Sets
3
Reps
6–8
Back-Off Sets — Drop to ~90% of today's top set. Controlled reps, full range. Quality over grind.
Rest 2.5–3 min

If top set hits 5 clean reps with strong back-offs → add +5 lb next heavy day. If you miss 5 reps → repeat the same weight. Three consecutive failures → drop 10 lb and rebuild.

Day B Volume Day
Sets
4
Reps
6–8
Volume Sets — 75–82% of your most recent top set. Smooth, consistent reps. Build work capacity.
Rest 2–3 min

Once all 4 sets reach 8 clean reps → add +5 lb on the next volume day. This tracks independently from heavy day progression — don't rush it.

Progression Rules
01
Top Set Success
Hit 5 clean reps and back-offs feel strong → add +5 lb next heavy session.
02
Top Set Miss
Fail to hit 5 reps → repeat the exact same weight next heavy session. Normal.
03
Stall Reset
3 consecutive failures at the same weight → drop 10 lb and rebuild momentum.
04
Volume Progression
All 4 sets hit 8 reps → +5 lb next volume day. Tracked separately from heavy day.

Bench 2× per week  ·  Alternate A → B → A → B  ·  48+ hrs between sessions
